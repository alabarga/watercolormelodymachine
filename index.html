Watercolor melody machine

WCMM is the result of a creative process making use of Machine learning. This machine is a artificial intelligence improvising at the piano while he paints on a canvas.

WCMM is based performance_rnn from Magenta endowing of a certain sensitivity to improvisation.

That the interpreter is sensitive and generates emotions is up to you, He just wants to tell stories, he offers you four with one result. unique and unrepeatable. It is in your hands to listen to each story of or mix them with each other.

Which one do you start with?

Created by karlosgliberal. All of the processing happens locally in the browser using Magenta.js, TensorFlow.js and P5.js
